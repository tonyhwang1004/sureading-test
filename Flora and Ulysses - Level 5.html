Flora and Ulysses

Kate DiCamillo
Ten-year-old Flora Belle Buckman is a self-proclaimed cynic who finds herself changed forever when she witnesses the neighbor accidentally vacuum up a squirrel with a powerful Ulysses vacuum cleaner. The squirrel, miraculously brought back to life, develops superpowers including strength, flight, and the ability to write poetry. This 2014 Newbery Medal winner combines humor, heart, and hope as Flora discovers the possibility of believing in extraordinary things and the promise of a capacious heart.
