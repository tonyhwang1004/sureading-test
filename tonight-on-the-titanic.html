Tonight on the Titanic

Mary Pope Osborne
Titanic trouble! Jack and Annie are in for an exciting, scary, and sad adventure when the Magic Tree House whisks them back to the decks of the Titanic on April 14, 1912. They find a mysterious puppy named Teddy under a spell, and to break it, they must collect four special gifts. The first gift must come from a ship lost at sea. As they explore the "unsinkable" ship, they meet children William and Lucy and experience the luxury of first-class life. But when the ship hits an iceberg and begins to sink, Jack and Annie must help their new friends find safety in the lifeboats while racing against time to escape the tragic disaster themselves.
