The Cricket in Times Square

George Selden (Illustrated by Garth Williams)
Chester Cricket accidentally travels from his peaceful Connecticut meadow to the bustling Times Square subway station in New York City. There he meets Tucker Mouse and Harry Cat, who become his loyal friends and guides to city life. When Mario Bellini, a young boy who helps run his family's newsstand, discovers Chester's remarkable musical talent, the little cricket becomes famous throughout the city. This 1961 Newbery Honor winner is a heartwarming tale about friendship, home, and discovering your special gifts.
