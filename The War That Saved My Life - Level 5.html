The War That Saved My Life

Kimberly Brubaker Bradley
Ten-year-old Ada has never left her one-room London apartment due to her abusive mother's shame over Ada's clubfoot. When WWII evacuations begin, Ada escapes with her younger brother Jamie to the English countryside, where they're reluctantly taken in by Susan Smith. Through learning to ride Susan's pony Butter, Ada discovers freedom, friendship, and her own worth. This Newbery Honor-winning tale explores themes of resilience, healing from trauma, and finding family in unexpected places during wartime Britain.
