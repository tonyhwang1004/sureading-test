Alone in His Teacher's House

Louis Sachar
Marvin's friends think he's the luckiest boy in the world when his teacher Mrs. North asks him to dog-sit for a week at her house! He gets to earn $25 if nothing goes wrong, and he gets to be alone in his teacher's house! But when Mrs. North's old dog Waldo starts acting sick and won't eat, Marvin isn't sure he'll get that bonus. In fact, Mrs. North may never talk to Marvin again! This funny and touching story shows Marvin learning about responsibility, friendship, and growing up.
